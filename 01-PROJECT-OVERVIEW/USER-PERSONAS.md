# User Personas

## Primary Personas

### 1. Emily - The Busy Professional
**Age**: 32
**Occupation**: Marketing Manager
**Location**: Urban (NYC, SF, London)
**Income**: $75,000-$120,000

**Goals**:
- Manage work stress and prevent burnout
- Develop better emotional regulation
- Find 10 minutes daily for self-reflection
- Track mood patterns related to work

**Pain Points**:
- No time for long journaling sessions
- Overwhelmed by blank page syndrome
- Needs structure but not rigidity
- Wants insights without data overload

**Tech Behavior**:
- iPhone user, checks phone 100+ times/day
- Uses productivity apps (Notion, Todoist)
- Willing to pay for quality tools
- Values privacy and data security

**Stoic.af Usage**:
- Quick morning intentions (2-3 min)
- Evening reflection during commute
- Weekly AI summaries on Sunday
- ChadGPT in "Reality Check" mode

**Quote**: "I need something that fits into my life, not another thing to manage"

## Anti-Personas (Who We're NOT Designing For)

### 1. The Therapist Seeker
- Needs clinical mental health support
- Looking for medical advice
- Severe mental health conditions
- Requires crisis intervention

### 2. The Data Hoarder
- Wants to journal novels daily
- Needs complex formatting options
- Requires infinite customization
- Wants to store multimedia libraries

### 3. The Social Sharer
- Wants public journaling
- Needs social features primarily
- Looking for dating/networking
- Requires constant validation

---

## Persona-Driven Design Decisions

### For Emily (Busy Professional)
- Quick entry templates
- Voice-to-text option
- Smart notifications
- Executive dashboard view

### For Jay (Student/Creative)
- Generous free tier
- Student discount codes
- Creative prompt variations
- Export for creative projects

### For Sam (Parent)
- Offline-first design
- Simple, clear interface
- Family-appropriate content
- Backup reminders

### Cross-Persona Needs
- 2-minute minimum viable session
- Works without internet
- Respects privacy absolutely
- Provides value immediately
- Grows with user journey
